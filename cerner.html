Project Overview

Healthcare aims to move away from a reactive approach towards a more proactive one, which helps to keep patients healthier, happier, and supports larger initiatives like population health. Our team will aid in these efforts through research to determine a list of product requirements for a patient portal. This research will be conducted through primarily competitive analysis and client interviews, as well as some practitioner interviews. These insights will then be synthesized to create a research report to hand off to our sponsors. Finally, utilizing skills in visual and interaction design, we will deliver high fidelity mockups of a web-based patient portal. This portal will not only provide 2-way communication between healthcare providers and patients, but also address the pain points we found in our research.
