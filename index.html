about s.deak

“All we wanna know is where the stars came from, but do we ever stop to watch them shine?”– Jon Bellion

Hi there, and welcome to my portfolio! My name is Sarah Deak, and I am currently a senior at Purdue University. Throughout the User Experience Design program, I have worked to develop specialties in front-end development and visual design. My passion for human-centered design has stemmed largely from a desire is to minimize the frustrations that have arisen throughout technology over the past few years. Through small but mighty changes in design, I intend to change the world for the better of many- which I hope one day includes many disciplines and spaces throughout the world.

Outside of school, I can be found reading a wide variety of books 📚, volunteering at the local dog shelters 🐶, formulating my next travel plans🚀, as well as working in customer service.🦸

Currently, I hope and plan to obtain a permanent position as a front-end developer at an altruistic growth company. This will provide me the opportunity to use my well-developed skills in UX, visual design, and web development to provide small but essential differences in the lives of many. Please reach out to sarahedeak@gmail.com to contact me for any inquires.
